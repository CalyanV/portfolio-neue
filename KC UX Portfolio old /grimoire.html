What is the core problem?

With the introduction of AI, the world has entered a new phase in the human history and an interface to support that progress is a necessity. AI models can perform various tasks and each of them excel in a single task. A singular interface that brings multiple mediums together is needed to build better products.

My Goal

Goal # 1: With the future of interfaces in mind, design a concept for a product that can integrate various AI models in one place.

GoalÂ #Â 2: The product must be scalable and facilitate cross functionality to solve complex problems with ease.
